I also enjoy spending time with my little brother, balancing my academic pursuits with family life, and embracing the joy of learning and growing together. I see programming not just as a skill but as a way to build, create, and innovate. Whether it’s writing code that makes life easier, developing projects that push boundaries, or simply understanding how technology works, I am always eager to learn more. My goal is to continue expanding my knowledge, refining my abilities, and contributing to the dynamic world of technology in meaningful ways.
